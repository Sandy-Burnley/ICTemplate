Animals and Society

This course is developed by Assistant Professor Dr. Seven Mattes. Dr. Mattes received her BA in Anthropology and Environmental Studies from WMU in 2007 and her PhD in Cultural Anthropology from Michigan State University in 2018. Specializing in Japanese human-animal relationships, her applied research is aimed at improving disaster preparedness and resiliency for animals through policy change and direct action with non-profit organizations. In both research and teaching, she emphasizes the significance of valuing and understanding human-animal entanglements across cultures.
